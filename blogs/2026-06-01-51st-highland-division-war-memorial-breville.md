It commemorates the casualties from elements the [[51st (Highland) Division]] of the British Army in actions immediately after [[D-Day]] during the [[Second World War]]. It was reported in late May 2026 that a bronze statue of a Scottish [[bagpipes|piper]], the largest element of the memorial, had been [[metal theft|stolen]].
